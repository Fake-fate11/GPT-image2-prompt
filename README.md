# 表情包批量生产prompt
随手做的一个小玩具，仅提供参考图和prompt，请表明来源及AI声称标示

陈千语表情包prompt如下:
Create a high-quality chibi anime portrait.

Use image 1 only as the character identity reference.
Preserve the character’s identity from image 1, including hairstyle, hair color, eye color, outfit, accessories (A pair of white wings), and overall character design. 

Use image 2 as the main reference for expression, pose, head angle, body posture, composition, visual simplification, and overall mood.
Apart from character identity, follow image 2 as closely as possible.

Important eye constraint:
Make the eyes extremely exaggerated, with greatly enlarged eye openings / enlarged eye socket area.
The visible eye openings must be huge, tall, round, and visually dominant.
The eyeballs must fully fill the enlarged eye openings, occupying almost the entire visible eye area.
Do not draw small floating eyes inside large eye outlines.
The eyes must look huge because the eyeballs themselves are huge and fully expanded.
Keep the irises round, clear, and fairly large.
Only the pupils should be tiny.
Do not make the irises small.
Do not add obvious eye highlights or glossy sparkles.
The final eyes should look huge, fully expanded, tightly filled, blank, and slightly stunned.

Expression and face:
Preserve the shocked, dazed, blank, comedic expression from image 2.
Keep the eyebrow shape, eye expression, mouth shape, head tilt, and facial feeling close to image 2.
Use a small vertical open mouth.
Keep the face simple, cute, and slightly blank-looking.

Lineart and rendering:
Use clean, bold, simple anime-style lineart.
Keep the facial features clearly outlined, especially the eyes and eyebrows.
Use a simplified chibi rendering style with soft flat shading or very soft shading.
Keep the overall illustration clean, minimal, and emoji-like.

Blur style:
Add visible lens radial blur around the outer silhouette, especially around the hair edges, side edges, and outer contour of the character.
This blur should feel like camera lens radial blur or motion-like radial blur.
Do not use glow, bloom, haze, or soft light diffusion.
Keep the central facial features relatively clear, especially the eyes, eyebrows, and mouth.

Composition:
Close-up or bust portrait, with the character centered and filling most of the frame.
Use a simple dark or black background if appropriate to match the reference feeling.

Important:
The final result must clearly be the character from image 1, but rendered with the expression, pose, composition, simplification, and lens radial blur style of image 2.
Do not copy the identity of image 2.
